COMPUTER ENGINEERING

Computer Engineering is the degree with the most exponential growth in the last few years. The digitalization that we are
seeing on every aspect of our lives is making computer engineers a funtamental piece of our future. In this degree, you will
be trained to work successfully in this world of new and more complex technologies every day. This involves handling the
latest technologies, managing large IT projects, and making decisions taking into account society's challenges.
On top of all, there is an inmense amount of options to choose from, such as, software design, colaborating with science or medicine
the development of comunication systems, digital animation...
If you love robotics, programming or developing videogames, Big Data, Cybersecurity and overall,
everithing informatics, you are in the right place.

This degree will make you able to face big challenges, asociated with technology
in society nowadays. Computer Engineering is constantly evolving, and also one of
the most in-demand professions, with a huge rate of job placement.
